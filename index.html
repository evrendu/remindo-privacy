Privacy Policy for Remindo

Last Updated: December 15, 2025
Effective Date: December 15, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Remindo (“we,” “our,” or “the app”). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how your information is handled.

Core Principle: Remindo is a local-first, privacy-focused application. Your personal data stays on your device.

Information We Collect

Data Stored on Your Device

  • Tasks, events, and reminders
  • Notes and journal entries
  • App preferences (theme, notifications, appearance)
  • Recent AI chat history (stored locally)
  • Daily AI message count for free-plan limits

AI Feature Usage

When you use the AI assistant, we process only what’s needed to respond:

  • Message text you enter
  • Limited recent context
  • Current date and timezone

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • No personal identification data
  • No user accounts or login information
  • No analytics or advertising data
  • No tracking
