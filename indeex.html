Privacy Policy for Remindo

Last Updated: December 15, 2025
Effective Date: December 15, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Remindo (“we”, “our”, or “the app”).

Your privacy is a core value of Remindo. We designed the app as a local-first, privacy-focused personal assistant. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use Remindo.

Key principle:
Your personal data stays on your device.
We do not operate user accounts, analytics systems, advertising networks, or cloud storage for user content.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Data Stored Locally on Your Device

Remindo stores the following information only on your device:

  • Tasks, events, and reminders
  • Notes and journal entries
  • App preferences (theme, notification settings, appearance)
  • Recent AI chat history (stored locally for usability)
  • Daily AI message count (used to enforce free-plan limits)

This data is stored using Apple’s secure iOS storage technologies and is not transmitted to our servers.

2.2 AI Feature Usage

When you use the AI assistant, the following information is processed only to fulfill your request:

  • Message text you enter
  • Limited recent context for conversation continuity
  • Current date and timezone (to interpret phrases such as “tomorrow”)

We do NOT send:

  • Your name, email, or contact details
  • Device identifiers
  • Location data
  • Contacts or calendars
  • Stored task or reminder history

Messages are processed in real time and are not stored by us.

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

Remindo does not collect:

  • Personal identification information
  • User accounts or login credentials
  • Location data
  • Analytics or usage tracking data
  • Advertising or marketing data
  • Cross-app or cross-site tracking information

3. How Information Is Used

Information handled by the app is used only to:

  • Display your tasks, reminders, and notes
  • Schedule local notifications
  • Provide calendar views and widgets
  • Interpret natural-language commands via AI

The AI assistant only helps interpret your input. All final task creation and management remain under your control.

4. Subscriptions & Payments

Remindo offers an auto-renewable monthly subscription.

  • Payment is charged to your Apple ID account at confirmation of purchase
  • Subscriptions automatically renew unless canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the current billing period
  • You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time in your Apple ID account settings
  • Any unused portion of a free trial, if offered, is forfeited when a subscription is purchased

Important: We do not store or process payment information. All billing, renewals, cancellations, and refunds are handled exclusively by Apple.

5. Data Storage & Security

  • All user data is stored locally on your device
  • Data is protected by iOS security features (Face ID, Touch ID, device passcode)
  • If iCloud Backup is enabled, your data may be included in Apple’s system backups
  • All AI communications use encrypted HTTPS connections

6. Third-Party Services

Remindo uses the following third-party services:

6.1 OpenAI API

  • Purpose: Natural language processing
  • Data shared: Message text only
  • Data usage governed by OpenAI’s API policies
  • API data is not used to train AI models

6.2 Apple Services

  • In-App Purchases
  • Notifications
  • Widgets

We do not use analytics SDKs, advertising networks, social media integrations, or third-party trackers.

7. Your Rights & Choices

Because your data is stored locally:

  • You can view, modify, or delete your data at any time
  • You can delete all data by removing the app from your device
  • You can disable AI features and continue using manual task creation

8. Children’s Privacy

Remindo does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app contains no advertising, tracking, or social features.

9. International Users

Remindo is available worldwide. AI messages may be processed on servers located in different countries, but all processing is encrypted and transient.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in legal requirements or app functionality. The “Last Updated” date will always indicate the most recent version.

11. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions:
Email: support@remindo.app
Support: Available through the App Store

12. Summary

  • No user accounts
  • No tracking or analytics
  • No advertising
  • Data stays on your device
  • Payments handled by Apple
  • You control your data
